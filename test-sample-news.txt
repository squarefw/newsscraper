Apple Inc. Announces Record Q4 Earnings with $95.3 Billion Revenue

Apple Inc. today reported its financial results for the fourth quarter ended September 30, 2025, setting a new company record with revenue of $95.3 billion. The technology giant exceeded Wall Street expectations, driven by strong iPhone 15 sales and robust services growth. CEO Tim Cook highlighted the company's continued innovation in AI features and expanding presence in emerging markets as key factors for this outstanding performance.
